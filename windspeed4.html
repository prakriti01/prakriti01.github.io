City Latitude v/s Wind speed


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------



Air pressure and winds depends indirectly on latitude, it receives less sunlight and a rise in latitude leads to a reduction in air pressure and a drop in latitude means that air pressure increases because of the weight of the air column. The amount of deflection varies with latitude, the speed of the object and the rotation of the earth. The deflection is greatest at the poles and decreases to zero at the equator. There is no direct correlation between wind speed and latitude.However, the scatterplot shows that most of the cities have wind speed of atleast 5mphs or less
