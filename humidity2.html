City Latitude v/s Humidity

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------



Humidity and lattitude is corelated with eachother. Relative humidity changes when temperatures change. Because warm air can hold more water vapor than cool air, relative humidity falls when the temperature rises if no moisture is added to the air. The scatter plot shows most of the cities have humidity of 60% or higher.
